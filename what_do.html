Community Baking

What we do on Saturdays:

  • Make dough
  • Oil pans and transfer dough to pans
  • Let it rise (1 hr)
  • Add toppings
  • Bake (25 min)
  • Cut into servings
  • Wrap and label
  • Repeat the above steps until about 4:30pm
  • Make dinner
  • Eat dinner
  • Clean up
  • Deliver
