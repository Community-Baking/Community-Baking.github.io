Community Baking

Everyone is Welcome

No experience necessary.

Kids are welcome, and there is plenty of room for them to play:

  • in "Fellowship Hall", which is a large, open room adjacent to the kitchen,
  • in a small play area, also adjacent to the kitchen, designed for children 0-6 years old, that has books, stuffed animals, Legos, etc.,
  • and in a small, fenced, and partly shaded yard outside the church.
Kids are also welcome to participate in any and all work that they can do safely, including mixing ingredients, wrapping bread, stamping and taping labels.

The space is wheelchair accessible.

No ability necessary. Even if you are unable to participate in any of the baking process, you are still welcome to come and participate in the community. Seriously, baking is only half of connumity baking.

Please bring your friends and family!
