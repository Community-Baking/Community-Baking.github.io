Community Baking

Gather. Bake. Share.

At Community Baking, friends and neighbors gather to bake focaccia bread for people who are hungry.

The Fall season of Community Baking starts Saturday September 13th! That first session is 9 am to 12:30 pm, while all other sessions will be in the afternoons. See the full schedule here: Schedule

This season we will be making a community dinner after baking bread (except on Septeber 13th). Dinner will be a buffet with pasta station, fresh fruit station, and garden salad station. Please bring any ingredients you would like!
