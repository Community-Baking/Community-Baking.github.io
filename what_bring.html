What to Bring

You don't have to bring anything!

If you want to, bring any of the following:

  • Toppings cheese, herbs, tomatoes, onions, or anything else you like
  • Snacks
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil - we use about 1 liter a week
  • A Stand Mixer
  • A Kitchen Scale
  • Dinner Ingredients - Dinner will be a buffet with a pasta station, fresh fruit station, and garden salad station. Please bring any ingredients you would like!
