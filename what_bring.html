What to Bring

You don't have to bring anything!

If you want to you can bring any of the following:

  • Wheat Flour We usually use bread flour, but whole wheat and all-purpose flour are also good. We often buy from King Arthur Bakingn Company becuase it is employee-owned and based in New England.
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Yeast We usually use instant yeast, but dry active yeast is fine too
  • Toppings Any toppings you would like to add, like cheese, herbs, tomatoes and onions
  • Snacks
  • Stand Mixer
  • Kitchen Scale
